Welcome to Beans & Beers Torquay

A coastal, family-friendly cafe and restaurant just minutes from the beach.

All day coffee

wide range Special dietary options (Vegan, GF, DF)

Kid friendly with dedicated play areas and Dog Friendly

Candle light / Date night Dinners

Experience the best of Torquay’s coastal lifestyle at Beans & Beers, your new local morning-to-night destination.

Located in the heart of Torquay, Beans & Beers is a relaxed coastal café, restaurant, and bar serving fresh Aussie eats with gluten-free and vegan options, specialty coffee, and ice-cold beers. We offer an extensive range of Cocktails, Mock-tails, Wines, and Spirits, alongside top-quality coffee sourced from Naked Espresso Roasters. Whether you’re stopping by for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or drinks, our space is designed for everyone to enjoy great food, beverages, and the laid-back Surf Coast vibe.

We’re proud to be one of the most family-friendly cafes in Torquay, dedicated play zone completed with a cubby house and sandpit, plus dog-friendly outdoor seating so the whole family can feel welcome.
