  • Home

Where Passion Meets Plate

What started in cookery school in 2020 became something far greater than just a café. When Hari and Yash met through their shared love for food, wine, and coffee, they discovered a dream rooted in creativity, connection, and community.

From experimenting with global flavors to crafting unforgettable celebration experiences, Beans & Beers Torquay was born from the belief that dining should feel magical — not just memorable.

Today, we’re a coastal, family-friendly café serving fresh Aussie eats, quality coffee, cold beers, and good vibes from morning till night. Whether it’s breakfast with friends, a romantic date night, or a special celebration, we create moments that feel warm, welcoming, and full of heart.

Because here, it’s never just about the food — it’s about the experience.
