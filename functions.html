Celebrate With Us:

At Beans and Beers Torquay, we believe every celebration should be as unique as the people you’re celebrating. Whether it’s a romantic date night, a milestone birthday, an anniversary, or a special surprise, we can help make it unforgettable.

Want the whole vibe set up for you with candles, fairy lights, balloons, and décor so you can walk in with your loved ones and amaze them? We’ve got you covered. Even if it’s an intimate celebration for two, a large gathering, or renting out the entire venue for a wedding or private event, we can tailor a package that fits your needs. we can take care of the back-end planning, decorate the venue, and ensure everything is ready for a foolproof surprise.

From a beautifully crafted cake to a customised gift, or even someone to perform and sing your feelings, we can bring your vision to life. At Beans and Beers, there are no set packages - just customised experiences designed to suit your vision and budget. Let us help you celebrate in style and create memories that will last a lifetime.
